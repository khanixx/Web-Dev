Why choose Kazakhstan?

Kazakhstan is more than just a dot on a map. It's a concentration of contrasts that will surprise even the most seasoned traveler:

  • Natural Diversity: From the cosmic landscapes of the Charyn Canyon and the turquoise Kolsay Lakes to the singing dunes of the Altyn-Emel National Park.
  • The Spirit of Nomads: Experience true hospitality in a real yurt, try traditional beshbarmak, and learn the secrets of ancient eagle hunters.
  • Cities of the Future and the Past: Compare the ultra-modern skyline of Astana with the cozy, green streets of Almaty and the sacred architecture of Turkestan.
  • Accessibility and Comfort: Visa-free travel for many countries, modern hotels, and high-quality service combined with pristine wilderness.
